About AKKADIANZ'26

AKKADIANZ'26 is an initiative by the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering at SBM College of Engineering and Technology. Our mission is to foster hands-on learning, industry relevance, and real-world exposure for engineering students.

This technical symposium brings together students, educators, and professionals to explore cutting-edge technologies, share knowledge, and build connections. Through workshops, talks, and collaborative projects, we aim to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry applications.

Full symposium details, including schedule, keynote speakers, and additional workshops, will be revealed soon. Stay tuned.
